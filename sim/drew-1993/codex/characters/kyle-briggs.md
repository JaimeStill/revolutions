# Kyle Briggs

Kyle Briggs is the fifth-grade alpha --- the boy who establishes hierarchy by finding out who flinches. He tested Drew twice. Once with words, once with a shove. Drew did not flinch either time. He did not fight back, either. He simply stood there and waited, offering nothing --- no fear, no anger, no reaction Kyle could use. Kyle, finding no purchase, moved on to softer targets.

It was not bravery, exactly. It was something closer to calculation --- Drew's instinct that reacting would cost more than absorbing. Kyle is not a nemesis. He is a weather pattern Drew learned to stand in without getting wet. But the encounter mattered more than Drew knows: it confirmed his belief that steadiness is a kind of armor, that if you refuse to give the world a handle, it cannot grip you. This is a useful lesson and a dangerous one, and Drew has not yet encountered the situation where it fails.
